What makes Baby Asst so awesome?

Accounts

Flexible account management allowing you to add and remove family and care givers so you never miss a thing

Easy to Use

Simple and intuitive design enables easy entry of all of baby's important actions and quick navigation with the bare minimum clicks required

Minimal Nightglare

Baby Asst's dark background and color schemes are meant to be gentle to sentive and tired eyes particularly in low light settings

Insightful

As Baby Asst accumulates baby's actions and activities caregivers are presented with insightful trends and patterns specific to each child

Relax - Baby Asst has you covered!

Baby Asst is here to assist parents and caregivers with simple yet detailed record keeping of baby's care activities in an intuitive, mobile friendly, web application built for minimal night glare. Overtime Baby Asst will help parents and invited caregivers learn baby's patterns and habits ultimately leading to better care and understanding of baby.

  • Baths
  • Diaper Changes
  • Feedings (Breast and Bottle)
  • Sleep
  • Temperatures
  • Timers
  • Tummy Time
  • Weight
  • Looks Great on Any Device
